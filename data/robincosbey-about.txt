Robin Cosbey is a Research Data Scientist within the Artificial Integeligence 
and Data Analytics Division at Pacific Northwest National Laboratory. Her research 
interests comprise machine learning explainability and model evaluation including 
the identification of biases and their greater impacts. She contributes her skills 
in natural language processing, machine learning, and deep learning to several 
projects. Robin values opportunities for education and growth. As such, she lends 
her time to various teaching and mentorship engagements with colleagues as well as 
students at the high school- and college-level.

Robin received her Masters in Computer Science from Western Washington University in 2020. 
Her background is in applied deep learning, specifically within the text and audio domains. 
During her undergraduate studies, Robin received the Citizenship Award for her research, 
tutoring, and mentorship within the Computer Science Department.
